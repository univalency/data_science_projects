Studying Bitcoin and altcoin correlations

I have downloaded the tables with minute, hourly and daily information on BTC and ETH from https://www.CryptoDataDownload.com<\p>

Let's first load the Bitcoin tables and understand them

open/close gives us prices at the beginning/end of a given time period. High/low are the highest and lowest prices attained during a given time period, respectively.Unix - This is the unix timestamp or also known as "Epoch Time". We can use this to convert to your local timezone. Tradecount is simply the number of trades.

The tables have most recent dates on top, so let's reverse them for convenience. Following Machine Learning etiquette, we should think about normalizing data. As for prices, we can introduce another column which records relative price changes. It's called a return in trading.

It is interesting to see how trading volume is related to the price change (return). Oh a daily scale, correlation between absolute change and volume is quite low:
