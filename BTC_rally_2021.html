A Wyckoffian view on the 2021 BTC bull run.

A wyckoffian view on the 2021 bull run. The most recent bull run started in early February 2021 and ended almost 2 months later. Quite a few people noticed that it behaved almost in direct accordance with the Wyckoffian theory. In this article, we analyse it in detail. The concept of a Composite operator (C.O.) or composite person is central to Wyckoff. He proposed a concept where we should think of a single person operating on the market. This represents big institutional players (aka whales), whose behaviour is usually quite different from average retail traders. C.O. always acts to his own benefit, so he wants to buy low and sell high. C.O accumulates assets gradually, without causing too much market movement.
